InvoiceChain

Live on Stellar testnet · burcumengu.github.io/invoicechain

01
Problem

Small businesses are cash-starved while waiting to get paid.

  • SMEs sell on 30–90 day terms — their cash is locked in unpaid invoices, so they can't make payroll or restock.
  • Bank factoring is slow, expensive, and often closed to small firms.
  • Investors have no easy, transparent way to fund these receivables and earn a short-term, asset-backed return.
  • The whole process is opaque — no shared record of who owes what or who pays reliably.
02
Solution

Tokenize the invoice. Sell it at a discount. Settle on-chain.

InvoiceChain puts the entire lifecycle on Stellar, so a seller gets cash in seconds and an investor gets a verifiable claim — no bank, no paperwork.

Create→ Sell @ discount→ Settle→ Reputation

Investor

Everyone

Every settle/default updates an on-chain reputation score.

03
How it works

A clean, trustless state machine.

1 · Create

Seller mints an invoice on-chain (amount, due date, debtor, discount). Status: Listed.

2 · Buy

Investor pays the discounted price directly to the seller. Status: Funded.

3 · Settle

At maturity the payer transfers the full face value to the investor. Status: Settled → seller's reputation improves.

4 · Default

Owner-gated, with a grace period. Unpaid past-due invoices are flagged Defaulted → reputation penalty.

The marketplace never custodies funds — all transfers are direct, and every state change is authorized by the right party.

Three Soroban contracts + a React dApp — live on testnet.

Core registry: create · buy · settle · default · cancel · views. Holds no funds.

reputation

On-chain trust score, writes gated to the marketplace (cross-contract call).
